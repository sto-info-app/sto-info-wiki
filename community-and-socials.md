- [Bluesky: @startrekonline.info](https://bsky.app/profile/startrekonline.info) (updates and announcements)

## Support Development

If you would like to support ongoing development and hosting costs you can do so via [Patreon](https://patreon.com/STOinfo).

Community support helps sustain hosting, tooling, and continued improvements.

As a thank you, patron supporters' names will be added to the Supporters page (a single list; no amounts shown).

There are no paid features, no exclusive access, and no obligation to contribute. The project will remain free for everyone.

This donation will support the app's ongoing operations and maintenance, including infrastructure and operational costs.
