# Fleet Management

← Back to [Ideas and Concepts](../ideas-and-concepts.md)

## Overview

Fleet Management is intended to provide structured tooling for managing Star Trek Online fleets outside the game environment.

The focus is:

- Data tracking
- Organisational clarity
- Transparency
- Audit capability

This feature is not intended to replace in-game functionality, but to enhance administrative visibility.

---

## Core Capability Areas

Planned features may include:

- Fleet roster tracking
- Rank visibility and hierarchy overview
- Contribution tracking
- Fleet holdings tracking
- Activity summaries

---

## Data Handling Principles

Given the sensitivity of user-related information:

- Login attempts are logged
- Audit trails are retained for defined periods
- IP addresses are nullified after retention thresholds
- Data minimisation principles apply

Retention model (current direction):

- Audit records retained for 180 days
- IP addresses nullified after 90 days
