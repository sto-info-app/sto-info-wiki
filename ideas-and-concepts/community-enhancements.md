# Community Enhancements

← Back to [Ideas and Concepts](../ideas-and-concepts.md)

## Overview

Community Enhancements focus on improving visibility, engagement, and contributor recognition while maintaining governance standards.

---

## Potential Features

- Idea voting or ranking system
- Story Spotlight rotation
- Contributor recognition
- Limited social integration
- Community-driven feature suggestions

---

## Governance Considerations

- Moderation controls
- Abuse prevention
- Clear content ownership rules
- Respect for intellectual property boundaries

---

## Long-Term Potential Features

- Structured community recognition system
- Cross-feature integration with STO Stories
- Transparent roadmap visibility
- Controlled feedback loops
