# STO Stories

← Back to [Ideas and Concepts](../ideas-and-concepts.md)

## Overview

STO Stories is a proposed feature designed to provide a structured platform for community-created narrative content inspired by the legacy Foundry system in Star Trek Online.

The objective is not to recreate in-game functionality, but to:

- Preserve community storytelling
- Provide structured episodic content
- Support collaborative story arcs
- Showcase community creativity

---

## Core Structure

Each Story would include:

- Banner image (thin header style)
- Profile image
- Metadata (author, tags, created date, etc.)
- Ordered Chapters

Each Chapter would include:

- Cover image
- Structured written content
- Previous / next navigation
- Reader progress tracking

---

## Characters and Crew Model

Naming structure:

- Story
- Chapter
- Character (in-universe role)
- Crew (real-world contributor)
- Spotlight (featured story)

Characters represent narrative entities.

Crew represents contributors such as:

- Authors
- Voice actors
- Editors
- Video creators

Stories may support:

- Multiple contributors
- Cross-user arcs
- Linked story chains
- Featured Spotlight rotation

---

## Reader Features (Planned)

- Progress state (not started / in progress / complete)
- Chapter completion tracking
- Story ordering by index
- Cross-story linking

---

## Long-Term Potential Features

- YouTube or video integration for voiced content
- Community voting or spotlight ranking
- Multi-author series arcs
- Archive of Foundry-inspired works

---

## Design Considerations

- No infringement of Cryptic Studios or Paramount intellectual property
- Clear separation between in-game assets and community fiction
- Structured moderation controls
- Scalable content model
